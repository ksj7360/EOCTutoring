Naomi feels confident about Math





“Math has always been a problem, but now I am willing to challenge to solve difficult problems.”



Naomi is a high school student in Syracuse. She struggled with math for many years throughout her early school years. She began working with math tutors for a year and her problem solving skill has improved a lot. She realized there are many different approaches to solve the problem and it resolved my issue with math. Now Her math exam result show that she now gets As in math exams.








Math tutor's comment to Naomi:



In the beginning, Naomi struggled with math so she labeled herself “I am bad at math.” I wanted to see why she was struggling. Over the course of time we gave her many problem sets and worked on correcting each problem individually. After gathering all the problems she did not get right, we began to see the pattern of her making the same mistakes repeatedly on certain problems. From that moment on, we introduced her solve problems with new methods. We tutors were not solving and explaining every single questions individually, but we made her change the whole perspective to approach to the problems. Now she no longer says I am bad at math, but instead I have a different approach. She gradually grasped the mathematical logic behind the questions. Now I see her being confident with math and working with her was one of the most rewarding experiences.
