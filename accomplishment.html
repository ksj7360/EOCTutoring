Accomplishment

Syracuse Educational Opportunity Center has helped reducing unemployment rate in Syracuse. State of Department of Labor revealed that in June 2019, the unemployment rate in Syracuse area fell to 3.6%. It was the lowest rate for any June in 19 years. What a great accomplishment!

As shown in the bar graph titled “Unemployment Rate between NYC and Syracuse in June 2018-2019”, In 2019, New York City’s unemployment rate (4.2 %) remained the same as previous year, whereas Syracuse had higher unemployment rate in 2018 (4.3 %) than NYC but much lower rate in 2019 (3.6 %). This is a record breaking amount. Same low unemployment rate in June was observed in 2000, and lower rate than 3.6% was dated back to as early as 1990. EOC has helped and observed students succeeded thus far. Ever since we were founded, we have been watching students struggle, succeed, fail, challenge to strive for the better result. On the way to their journeys, we laughed, cried, discussed many things, and sought for better solutions altogether. It hasn’t been always easy. Nevertheless we want to continue to go on since our biggest joy is to see students succeed. Their victory is also our victory and that is our biggest reward. We want to make a difference in the world. A big difference.
